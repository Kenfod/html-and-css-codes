Typs of Cars

Hatchback
A hatchback is a type of passenger vehicle with a rear door that opens upwards, providing access to the cargo area. This design creates a more versatile cargo space compared to a traditional sedan, as the rear seats can often be folded down to increase storage. Hatchbacks are known for their practicality, fuel efficiency, and affordability.
Sedan
A sedan is a type of passenger car with a distinct three-box body design: the engine compartment, the passenger compartment, and the trunk. It's typically a four-door car with a separate trunk. The word "sedan" comes from the Latin word "sedere," meaning "to sit," reflecting the comfort and smooth ride associated with these vehicles.

MUV
A Multi-Utility Vehicle (MUV), also known as a minivan, is a type of car designed for both personal and commercial use. MUVs are typically spacious, accommodating a large number of passengers, and can be used for transporting goods or passengers for commercial purposes, as well as family use. They are generally more affordable and practical for daily city driving compared to SUVs, which are often geared towards off-roading and rugged terrain
Coupe
"Coupe" refers to a passenger car body style characterized by two doors and a sloping or truncated rear roofline. Coupes are generally considered to be smaller and sportier than sedans, with a focus on performance and style.
